MJM AI · DIA — Local counting runs entirely in your browser; AI mode sends image tiles to your own Gemini proxy for higher accuracy on dense maps. Green-detection works best on well-separated mature palms and tidy nursery rows; it can under-count where canopies overlap heavily or weeds are as green as the crop. Big maps are downsampled for speed and the count is scaled back to full resolution. A GeoTIFF gives real coordinates per palm; plain images give pixel positions.
